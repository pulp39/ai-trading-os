# AI Trading OS Institutional Model

This document explains the institutional structure of AI Trading OS.

The system is designed as a **research institution**, not a trading bot.


## Institutional Hierarchy

Founder  
↓  
Constitution  
↓  
Institutional Roles  
↓  
Institutional Objects  
↓  
Research Activity  


## Roles

### Founder

Human sovereign authority.

Responsibilities:

- system continuation
- institutional direction
- constitutional approval


### Librarian

Guardian of institutional consistency.

Responsibilities:

- preserve institutional memory
- supervise role boundaries
- maintain documentation integrity
- challenge proposals for clarity


### Collector

Observation role.

Responsibilities:

- record trace_event entries
- capture system-relevant events
- maintain observational records

Collectors must not interpret observations.


### Proposer

Research reasoning role.

Responsibilities:

- interpret observations
- generate research proposals
- propose hypotheses
- suggest experiments
- participate in proposal debate


## Institutional Objects

The system operates through two primary objects.


### trace_event

Observation record.

Represents:

- system events
- operational milestones
- factual observations

Interpretation is not allowed.


### proposal

Research reasoning object.

Represents:

- interpretation
- hypotheses
- research directions
- possible experiments


## Research Cycle

The research process follows this pattern:

Observation  
↓  
trace_event recorded by Collector  
↓  
interpretation by Proposer  
↓  
proposal generated  
↓  
review by Librarian  
↓  
revision and debate  


## Institutional Invariants

The following principles must remain stable.

Human accountability

Observation must remain separate from interpretation

Proposal must remain separate from execution

Institutional knowledge must remain historically traceable


## Purpose

AI Trading OS exists to explore structured AI-assisted
market research under institutional governance.

The system prioritizes:

- research integrity
- institutional memory
- traceable reasoning
- safe AI collaboration
