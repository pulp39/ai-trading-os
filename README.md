# README.md

AI Trading OS  
Experimental institutional research environment for AI-assisted market analysis.

---

## System Overview

AI Trading OS is an experimental research operating environment designed to explore AI-assisted market analysis under institutional governance.

The system emphasizes:

- traceable observation
- structured interpretation
- institutional memory
- role-based AI participation
- disciplined system evolution

The project is intentionally narrow in scope and prioritizes architectural clarity over rapid feature expansion.

---

## Authoritative Document

The governing document of this repository is:

- constitution.md

The constitution defines:

- institutional roles
- role boundaries
- institutional objects
- research governance

All system components and documentation must remain consistent with the constitution.

---

## Institutional Structure

AI Trading OS operates through a layered institutional structure.

Founder  
↓  
Constitution  
↓  
Institutional Roles  
↓  
Institutional Objects  
↓  
Research Activity  

Core roles include:

- Founder
- Librarian
- Collector
- Proposer

Role responsibilities and boundaries are defined in `constitution.md`.

---

## Institutional Objects

The system currently centers on two primary institutional objects.

trace_event  
Observation record capturing factual system events.

proposal  
Structured research reasoning generated by proposers.

This separation preserves the distinction between:

observation  
interpretation

and protects the integrity of the research process.

AI Trading OS should be understood as an institutional research system rather than a trading bot.
