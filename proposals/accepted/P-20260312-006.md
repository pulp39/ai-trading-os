## Scope Statement

This proposal establishes operational governance rules for Collector concurrency,
trace_event generation model, and Collector lifecycle management in AI Trading OS.

This proposal is not a constitutional amendment. Constitution Article 4 role
boundary (observation only, no interpretation) is preserved as the foundation.

## Part I — Collector Concurrency Model (Hybrid)

### Primary Collector

- Generates canonical_observation trace_events
- Designated by Founder after Stage evaluation (P-20260310-009)
- Current designation: base_collector_v1

### Secondary Collector

- Generates supplemental_observation trace_events
- Functions as fallback when Primary is unavailable

### Instrument Scope (supplementary concept)

Optional per-Collector instrument assignment to structurally prevent
observation conflicts.

## Part II — trace_event Generation Model (Model C)

Primary -> event_type: canonical_observation
Secondary -> event_type: supplemental_observation

metadata must include: collector_id, observation_class, instrument

No trace_event schema change required.

## Part III — Collector Lifecycle

| Event | Condition | Authority | trace_event |
|---|---|---|---|
| Temporary Suspension | Quality degradation / technical failure | Librarian | collector_suspended |
| Reactivation | Cause resolved | Librarian | collector_reactivated |
| Demotion (Primary->Secondary) | Better Collector designated / quality drop | Librarian recommendation + Founder approval | collector_demoted |
| Permanent Deactivation | Serious violation / role boundary breach | Librarian recommendation + Founder approval | collector_deactivated |
| Disqualification | Deactivation + no re-approval | Librarian recommendation + Founder approval + Founder Record | collector_deactivated (disqualified: true) |
| Primary Succession | System-critical operational change | Proposal -> Librarian review -> Founder approval | primary_collector_changed |

Disqualification does not delete institutional history. append-only principle applies.

## Part IV — Lifecycle trace_event Types

canonical_observation, supplemental_observation,
collector_suspended, collector_reactivated,
collector_demoted, collector_deactivated,
primary_collector_changed

## Limitations

- P-20260310-009 entry-stage evaluation remains in effect
- This proposal governs concurrent operation, not entry evaluation
