## 1. Research Hypothesis

Formally establishing a Collector Core seat as an institutional AiiD,
with OpenClaw as initial occupant on VM-A, will provide AI Trading OS
with a structured and governable Observation Layer that supports
proposal generation, research, and market awareness under the existing
governance framework.

## 3. Supporting Observations

- P-20260312-006 established the Collector Governance Framework,
  defining Primary and Secondary Collector roles, lifecycle events,
  and the hybrid collector model. The framework is operational but
  the Collector seat has not been formally defined as an AiiD.
- P-20260313-002 established AiiD as the formal institutional identity
  system. Collector must be integrated into this system to ensure
  consistent institutional traceability.
- The Founder policy directive specifies: collector_core on VM-A,
  OpenClaw as initial occupant, observation-only phase.
- Formalizing the Collector seat as an AiiD with bounded permissions
  ensures the observation-execution separation mandated by
  constitution Article 2 (Role Separation) and Article 8 (Invariants).

## 4. Proposal Content

### 4.1 Collector Core Seat Definition

A new AiiD is hereby established:

```
aiid:         collector_core
display_name: Collector Core
role:         Collector
layer:        Observation Layer
```

This AiiD represents an institutional seat within the Observation Layer.
The occupant is replaceable per constitution Article E and
AiiD Specification (P-20260313-002 section 4.1).

### 4.2 Initial Implementation (Phase A)

```
deployment_target: VM-A
initial_occupant:  OpenClaw
phase:             Phase A — Single Environment
```

Phase A is observation-only. No execution authority is granted
to the occupant during this phase.

### 4.3 Initial Permissions

**Allowed:**
- market observation
- data summarization
- hypothesis generation
- non-binding analysis reports

**Disallowed:**
- order execution
- repository modification
- trace_event creation
- proposal enactment

Observation records generated by the collector_core occupant
may be recorded as trace_events through the Registrar or
other institutional recording mechanisms. The collector_core
occupant itself does not possess direct trace_event write
authority during Phase A.

### 4.4 Relationship to P-20260312-006

P-20260312-006 defines the Collector Governance Framework including:
- Primary / Secondary Collector model
- Collector lifecycle (suspended / reactivated / demoted / deactivated)
- trace_event types for Collector state changes

This proposal does not modify P-20260312-006. It establishes
collector_core as the first formal AiiD-registered Collector seat,
operating under the governance rules defined in that framework.

Upon adoption, collector_core / OpenClaw is designated as the
**Primary Collector** under the P-20260312-006 hybrid model,
with canonical_observation as the observation class.

### 4.5 Future Expansion Path

The Collector seat may be expanded in future proposals to:

```
collector_core    (this proposal — Phase A)
collector_market  (future)
collector_macro   (future)
```

Future expansion requires separate proposals. This proposal
establishes collector_core only.

### 4.7 Institutional Layer Structure

Upon adoption, the institutional structure becomes:

```
AI Assembly (deliberation)
  ├ gpt_librarian   (Seat 1)
  └ claude_proposer (Seat 2)

Execution Layer
  └ claude_registrar

Observation Layer
  └ collector_core (OpenClaw / VM-A)
```

### 4.8 Relationship to Existing Framework

This proposal does not modify:
- the Constitution
- Founder authority
- the Proposal system
- Librarian review authority
- Registrar execution authority
- P-20260312-006 Collector Governance Framework

It extends the AiiD system (P-20260313-002) to cover the
Observation Layer and formally registers the first Collector seat.

## 6. Confidence Statement

High. This proposal directly implements the Founder policy directive
for collector_core / OpenClaw / VM-A within the established AiiD and
Collector Governance frameworks. Permission boundaries are conservative
and consistent with constitutional Role Separation principles.
Phase A two-tier observation recording model (collector generates
observation -> Registrar records trace_event) ensures institutional
log integrity during the initial deployment phase.

## 7. Limitations

- OpenClaw implementation details on VM-A are outside the scope of
  this governance proposal. Technical implementation is a separate
  operational matter.
- Phase A is observation-only. Any expansion of permissions or
  deployment targets requires a separate proposal.
- collector_market and collector_macro are noted as future expansion
  paths but are not defined here.
