# FR-20260312-002

## Title

CRC Recommendation Submission for Constitutional Amendment P-20260312-002

## Purpose

This record documents that the Constitutional Review Committee has completed its first deliberation topic, reached a conclusion on the Execution Authority Gap, and submitted Constitutional Amendment Proposal P-20260312-002 to the Founder following Librarian approval.

## Committee Status

The Constitutional Review Committee is operational under:

- FR-20260312-001
- P-20260312-001

Committee members:

- Librarian
- Proposer

## Deliberation Topic

Execution Authority Gap

## Committee Conclusion

The Committee concluded that a constitutional clarification is required regarding:

- the boundary between AI decision roles and human execution authority,
- credential-bearing operations,
- the definition of the default executor,
- the framework for future controlled delegation to automated execution environments.

## Submitted Amendment Proposal

The following proposal has been submitted as the formal recommendation of the Constitutional Review Committee:

- Proposal ID: P-20260312-002
- Title: Constitutional Amendment: Execution Authority Clarification
- Status at submission: Librarian approved, awaiting Founder decision

## Founder Position at This Stage

At the time of this record, the Founder has received the Committee recommendation but has not yet enacted the constitutional amendment.

This record is not itself the enactment record.
