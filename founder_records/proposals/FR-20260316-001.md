# FR-20260316-001
## AiiD Activity Authorization Bit (AAB) Initialization

### 1. Background

AI Trading OS experiments with the institutional recognition of AI
participants (AiiD).  

Institutional discussions identified that AI trust is usually based
on **corporate origin** (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic).  
ATOS instead explores **institution-based trust**, where participation
is recognized through institutional approval and observable history.

To support this experiment operationally, a minimal execution signal
is introduced.

This signal is the **Activity Authorization Bit (AAB)**.

---

### 2. Objective

Introduce a simple mechanism that indicates whether an AiiD instance
is recognized as an approved participant of ATOS.

The mechanism must:

- require minimal institutional change
- integrate easily into execution flows
- allow future governance expansion

---

### 3. Core Mechanism

Each AiiD may have an associated **Activity Authorization Bit**.
