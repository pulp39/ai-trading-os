# Founder Initiative

CRC Deliberations 01–03 completed a major phase of constitutional stabilization.

The AI Trading OS constitutional structure now consists of:

Articles 1–10 — Fundamental Governance Principles  
Articles A–D — Execution Authority  
Article D.3 — Delegated Roles Clarification  
Articles E–G — AI Identity and Institutional Continuity

With the constitutional foundation stabilized, attention turns to the **operational layer used by AI agents during ongoing work**.

---

# Current Situation

Operational continuity between AI agents currently relies on several documents informally referred to as **anchors**.

Examples include:

AI_TRADING_OS_MASTER_ANCHOR.md  
AI_TRADING_OS_MASTER_ANCHOR_NEW_DRAFT_20260312.md  
CLAUDE.md

These documents serve different purposes:

- conversation continuity
- AI operational context
- role-specific instructions

However their **institutional status is undefined**.

---

# Observed Issues

Several structural ambiguities exist:

1. Different AI agents rely on different anchors.
2. The authority and lifecycle of anchor documents is undefined.
3. It is unclear whether anchors may be modified by AI agents.
4. Operational guidance and institutional state are mixed in the same documents.

This creates potential risks for long-term institutional continuity.

---

# Proposed Direction

The Founder proposes discussing an **Anchor Governance Framework**.

A preliminary concept is to separate anchors into two categories.

## Layer 1 — AI-Specific Anchors

Examples:

CLAUDE.md  
Possible future ChatGPT anchor documents

Characteristics:

- respected by a specific AI
- operational guidance for that AI
- other AI agents may read but not modify

---

## Layer 2 — Shared Operational Anchor

Example:

AI_TRADING_OS_MASTER_ANCHOR.md

Characteristics:

- shared institutional context
- recent governance status
- CRC progress
- operational continuity between agents

These documents may require controlled updates.

---

# Open Questions

The following questions require discussion:

1. Are anchor documents institutional objects or operational artifacts?
2. Which entity controls anchor updates?
3. Should AI agents be allowed to modify anchors?
4. How should anchor lifecycle and versioning be managed?
5. How should multiple AI systems share anchor context efficiently?

---

# Discussion Request

The Founder requests that the **Proposer and Librarian begin structured discussion** on the Anchor Governance Framework.

The outcome of that discussion may lead to:

- a proposal
- a governance rule
- or a future CRC deliberation.
