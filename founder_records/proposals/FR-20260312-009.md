# Founder Decision

The Founder approves Proposal P-20260312-006 "Collector Governance Framework".

This decision establishes operational governance rules for Collector concurrency,
trace_event generation, and Collector lifecycle management within the AI Trading OS.

## Key Points

- Hybrid Collector Model (Primary / Secondary) is formally adopted
- canonical_observation / supplemental_observation trace_event model is adopted
- Collector Lifecycle governance (suspension, demotion, deactivation, disqualification,
  Primary Collector succession) is formally defined
- base_collector_v1 is designated as Primary Collector
- P-20260310-009 (AI Collector Evaluation Framework) remains in effect as the
  entry-stage governance; P-20260312-006 governs concurrent operation thereafter

No constitutional amendment is performed by this decision.

## CRC Status

CRC Deliberation 04 is formally closed by this Founder decision.
