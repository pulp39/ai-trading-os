# FR-20260312-001

## Title

Constitutional Review Committee Establishment and Appointment

## Purpose

Founder hereby establishes the Constitutional Review Committee as a formal preparatory body for future constitutional review and amendment drafting within AI Trading OS.

## Institutional Decision

The Constitutional Review Committee is hereby established.

Initial members are appointed as follows:

- Librarian
- Proposer

## Mandate

The Committee is authorized to:

1. identify constitutional gaps, risks, and ambiguities,
2. study amendment needs,
3. prepare draft constitutional amendment proposals,
4. submit recommendations to the Founder through the established governance process.

## Limits of Authority

The Committee does not have authority to:

- amend the constitution directly,
- approve constitutional changes by itself,
- alter role authority definitions without Founder approval,
- execute institutional changes outside the standard governance process.

## Immediate Context

This Committee is established in response to the recognition that future governance automation and AI role expansion require prior constitutional clarification, especially regarding:

- institutional self-modification limits,
- Founder authorization boundaries,
- automation boundaries for Registrar execution,
- secret-bearing execution and credential handling.

## Effect

This record takes effect immediately as a Founder institutional act.

## Notes

The Committee is a preparatory constitutional body.
All constitutional amendments remain subject to Founder decision and formal institutional processing.
