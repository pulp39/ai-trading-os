# FR-20260312-003

## Title

Constitutional Amendment Enactment — Execution Authority Clarification

## Purpose

This record documents the Founder’s formal decision to enact the constitutional amendment proposed in P-20260312-002 following recommendation by the Constitutional Review Committee and approval by the Librarian.

## Amendment

Proposal enacted:

P-20260312-002  
**Constitutional Amendment: Execution Authority Clarification**

The amendment introduces constitutional clarification regarding:

- the boundary between AI decision roles and human execution authority
- handling of credential-bearing operations
- definition of the default executor
- framework for controlled delegation to automated execution environments

## Process Confirmation

The amendment followed the constitutional governance process:

1. Gap identified by the Constitutional Review Committee
2. Amendment drafted by the Proposer
3. Librarian review completed and approved
4. Recommendation submitted to the Founder
5. Founder enactment

## Enactment

By this record the Founder formally **approves and enacts** the constitutional amendment defined in P-20260312-002.

The amendment becomes part of the governing constitution of the AI Trading OS.
